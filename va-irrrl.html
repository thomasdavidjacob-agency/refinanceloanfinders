VA IRRRL FAQ

Do I need an appraisal for a VA IRRRL?
In most cases, no appraisal is required for a VA IRRRL. The VA waives the appraisal requirement for streamline refinances, which speeds up closing significantly and eliminates the risk of a value issue. Some lenders may still require one — we'll let you know upfront.
Do I need to verify my income?
VA IRRRL has reduced documentation requirements. In many cases, you won't need full income verification — especially if you're lowering your payment. The exact requirements depend on the lender and your specific scenario. We'll tell you exactly what's needed.
Can I do a VA IRRRL if I no longer live in the home?
Yes — unlike a VA purchase loan, the IRRRL only requires that you previously occupied the home. If you've since moved out and the property is now a rental, you're still eligible for the streamline refinance.
What is the VA IRRRL funding fee?
The VA IRRRL funding fee is 0.5% of the loan amount — much lower than the 2.15–3.3% fee on purchase loans. Veterans receiving VA disability compensation are exempt from the funding fee entirely. The fee can be rolled into the new loan with no cash out of pocket.
